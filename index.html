WELCOME TO THE TAVERN OF INDEPENDENCE 

The Tavern of Independence is a wonderful place for top quality fare, cocktails and first-rate service all in a friendly atmosphere. Thinking of dining out with your family? Come and enjoy our charming, completely refurnished interior. After work drinks? We’re ideally located in the Cloverleaf area of Independence, and accessible to Interstates 77 and 480. We are also home to some of the greatest fans of Northeast Ohio and Buckeye athletics. Stop in and see our new tavern. You’ll be glad that you did.
